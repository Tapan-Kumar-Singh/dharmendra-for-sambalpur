SEVA SUSHASAN AND GARIB KALYAN

The Government led by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has completed nine years of ‘Seva, Sushasan and Garib Kalyan’. With PM Modi at the helm, India has witnessed an unprecedented shift towards people-centric and result-oriented governance. Over these nine years, we have witnessed numerous structural, tangible and intangible transformations covering every sector of our economy and across all sections of our society. Since 2014, India’s remarkable rise and its growing resilience – both at home as well as internationally – has been widely appreciated.

India, during the Amrit Kaal of her independence is more determined than ever to achieve the goal of a Viksit Bharat.
